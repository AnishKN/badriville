Description

We give you a homely experience and a very calm holiday stay, in the comforts of a Colonial Home, set amidst myriad attractions near the Joshimath. Wakeup to the splendid view of Sleeping Lady and Mount Elephant, with surrounding filled with greenery to give you a peaceful, calm and quiet stay.
